Welcome to a generic Wordle game.

How to play?

  • Guess the 5-letter word in 6 attempts.
  • Type your guess in each row and press Enter.
  • Correct guesses earn 5 points, and the game restarts with a new word.
  • Click the reset icon to restart the game at any time.
  • After all wrong guesses you'll see the right answer.
